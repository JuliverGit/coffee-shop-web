About Us

Remo's Coffee Shop is a student-made website designed to showcase a modern café concept that blends comfort, creativity, and great coffee. Our mission is to offer high-quality beverages and a relaxing space where customers can unwind, study, or connect with friends.

This project was developed by four IT students from USTP Villanueva as part of our Human Computer Interaction (HCI) course. Together, we combined our skills in design, coding, and creativity to bring this café concept to life.
